The last summer camp

a short animated film

lenght: 7 min

Two friends are spending summer together at a camp since their childhoods. We meet them during their last camp, both of them know that no other summer will be the same. Will their friendship survive that? Fulfilling their camp duties leads them to the necessity of destroying an anthill. This horrible work is interrupted by their argument. Before they can reconcile, thousands of ants begin to swarm around them with a desire for revenge...
